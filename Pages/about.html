WHY WAS "MADE EASY" MADE?

On February 15, 2017, Professor Drei Abmab of the University of Computer Science noticed that most available online courses in, relation to Computer Science for Kids, were not able to teach children to fully grasp the wonders and excitement of Computer Science. He noticed the learning curve in these platforms for a topic would start slowly and easy then suddenly start bombarding so much information all at once.


This would lead to two possible scenarios, either the child would not understand and get bored, or the child would have a harder time understanding and take much more time to learn than they had to. Using his 23 years of experience in teching Computer Science he, and 30 individuals with the same mindset, made "MADE EASY" to help young learners understanding the concepts, theorems, theory, processes, and mindset easier. After two years of development Made Easy was launched on April 1, 2019.
